How It Works

Built on match science,
not intuition.

tennisIQ uses a logistic regression model trained on 110,000+ ATP and WTA matches, incorporating surface-adjusted Elo ratings, serve and return profiles, fatigue signals, and head-to-head history.

01
Surface-Adjusted Elo
Standard Elo treats all matches equally. Our model builds separate hard, clay, grass, and indoor hard ratings — because a clay specialist with a 1900 clay Elo tells a very different story than a 1900 overall Elo.
4 surfaces
Separate Elo ratings per surface
02
Serve & Return Profiling
Every prediction is informed by 8 serve and return metrics per player, calculated across their full match history. 1st serve won %, 2nd serve won %, break point conversion, double fault rate — all surface-stratified.
8 metrics
Per-player, per-surface serve profile
03
Market Intelligence
Where bookmaker odds exist, we calculate closing line value — the gap between our model's fair probability and what the market implies. Positive CLV is the most reliable indicator of long-term edge.
